The Coelacanth Quartet

Eons ago, I decided to write a novel. I then decided, as a language nerd, I should create a number of constructed languages, and now I’ve decided to put this on the web. So I’m two levels away from actually writing the thing. One day….

The Coelacanth Quartet details the adventures of Corla, a Tsarin telepath far from home.

This quartet comprises a cycle of four stories that can be enjoyed starting at any point.
