The Crackled Egg

The Crackled Egg takes place in Zhalad. Two races of people live there. While two Zhaladi or two Fezhel can easily telepathically receive and project emotion amongst themselves, a Zhaladi encountering a Fezhel has no such ability. Thus the two races tend to live separately.

Vedran, advisor to a weak ruler, hears of a device capable of increasing one’s emotional projection abilities to such a degree that one could lead a country’s people by their hearts. He attempts to track down this device, with the hope of displacing his king.
