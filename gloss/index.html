Gloss

Three-tail-having Fish-called Story-four-gen Ryan-gen Eakins.

A “threetail-having fish” is a coelacanth, referring to the three lobes on the tail of members of this species. A “feather-becoming egg” is a rough translation of crackled egg, and refers to a hatchling emerging into the world.
